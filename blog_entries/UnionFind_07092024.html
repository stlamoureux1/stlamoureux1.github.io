Data Structures 1: Presenting Union-Find

Find my presentation here (zipped pdf)

Over Summer 2024 I participated in a seminar on Data Structures and Algorithms in practice led by Jenweil Yang. A big part of our emphasis was technical communication and "soft-skills" (which can be pretty hard, it turns out). We had some fruitful discussion on the presentation itself that really drove home the importance of visualizations and careful walk-throughs of examples. Any future revision of this presentation will be less wordy and more picture-y.

I plan to follow this post up with a tutorial and some illustrations of leetcode problems that benefit from the use of a Union-Find. Any faults or inaccuracies are my own. Please let me know if you spot them.
