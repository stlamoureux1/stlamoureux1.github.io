About Me

I'm currently studing math and computer science at Laney College in Oakland, with a lot of extra-curricular learning on the side. My primary interests are systems programming and high-performance computing.

I originally studied linguistics at UC Berkeley, where I got my Bachelor's and Master's degrees. It was through linguistics I got my first exposure to formal language theory, lambda calculi, type theory, and abstract algebra. I've been hooked ever since.

In addition to my studies, I work as an in-home care provider to my nephew, who has special needs. As he gets older and needs me less, I am looking to transition into an engineering career.
